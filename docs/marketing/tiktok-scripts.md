## Production Tips

1. **Use CapCut** — Free, powerful, trending templates
2. **Keep it under 30 seconds** — Attention spans are short
3. **Hook in first 3 seconds** — Otherwise they scroll
4. **Use Nepali context** — Reference load-shedding, NEB stress, coaching costs
5. **Post consistently** — Algorithm rewards regular posting
6. **Engage in comments** — Reply to every comment in first hour
7. **Cross-post** — Same content works on Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts
8. **Track metrics** — Watch time, saves, shares > likes

---

*Create 10-15 of these per week. Batch film on weekends. Post daily.*
