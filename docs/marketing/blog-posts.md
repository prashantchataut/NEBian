   - "I hate Chemistry so I'll focus on Physics"
   - Solution: Weak subjects = biggest score improvement opportunity

3. Mistake 3: Passive Reading
   - "Highlighting everything = highlighting nothing"
   - Solution: Active recall, practice questions, teach others

   - "I'll solve them after finishing the syllabus"
   - Solution: Start past papers from Month 2

5. Mistake 5: All-Night Cramming
   - "Sleep deprivation destroys memory consolidation"

   - Solution: Grammar needs practice too

8. Mistake 8: Perfectionism
   - "I need to understand EVERYTHING before moving on"
   - Solution: 80% understanding + move on > 100% understanding + stuck

9. Mistake 9: No Error Analysis
   - "I got it wrong, oh well"
   - Solution: Error notebook — review weekly

10. Mistake 10: Wrong Resources
    - "Using outdated or inaccurate notes"
    - Solution: Teacher-verified, syllabus-aligned materials

11. Conclusion
    - "Avoid these 10 mistakes and you're already ahead of 80% of students."

## SEO Best Practices

1. **Title tags:** Include target keyword near the beginning
2. **Meta descriptions:** 150-160 characters, include CTA
3. **Headers:** Use H1 for title, H2 for sections, H3 for subsections
4. **Internal links:** Link to other blog posts and app pages
5. **External links:** Link to NEB official site, credible sources
6. **Images:** Use descriptive alt text ("NEB Grade 12 Physics syllabus breakdown")
7. **URL structure:** `/blog/keyword-rich-slug`
8. **Mobile-friendly:** Short paragraphs, clear fonts
9. **Page speed:** Optimize images, minimal JavaScript
10. **Schema markup:** Article schema for rich snippets
