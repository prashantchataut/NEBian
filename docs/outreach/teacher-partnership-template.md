## Tips for Outreach Success

1. **Personalize every message** — Mention the specific school, subject, or achievement that made you reach out to them.

2. **Lead with value, not ask** — Show how they or their students benefit before asking for anything.

3. **Be respectful of hierarchy** — In Nepal, teachers command deep respect. Use "sir/ma'am" and formal language unless they indicate otherwise.

4. **Follow up 2-3 times** — Many teachers are overwhelmed. A polite follow-up after 1 week and 3 weeks is acceptable.

5. **Offer a low-commitment first step** — "Can I send you a sample?" is easier than "Can you create content for us?"

6. **Use Nepali when appropriate** — If the teacher is more comfortable in Nepali, switch to Nepali. It builds trust.

7. **Leverage warm introductions** — A referral from a known teacher or school principal is 10x more effective than cold outreach.
